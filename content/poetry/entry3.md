here i am,\
waiting for your call,\
thinking through it all,\
feeling as our hearts fall

close your eyes,\
pressing skin to skin,\
asking what you mean,\
living is not a sin

this is for you,\
don't be sad at all,\
let's face it through it all,\
love will be waiting for your call
